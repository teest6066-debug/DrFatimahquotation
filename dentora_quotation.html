Growth Roadmap

In the early phase, we invest fully in organic content to build a trusted, high-authority presence before activating paid advertising. A strong organic base lowers ad costs and converts far more efficiently.

01

Content-First Phase (Months 1–3)

Maximum organic content volume. Build authority, trust, and algorithm momentum. Zero paid ad spend.

02

Transition Phase (Months 4–6)

Organic content scales down. Paid campaigns introduced. Monthly subscription adjusts proportionally.

03

Full-Funnel Phase (Month 6+)

Balanced content + ads. Compounding ROI as organic trust amplifies paid performance.
